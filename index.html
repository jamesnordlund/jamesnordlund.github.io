James Nordlund

I am a financial economist at Louisiana State University and my principal research interest is in network/peer/spillover effects. I currently serve as the Ph.D. program coordinator for the Finance Department. I am also the creator and sysadmin for the CloudLab computing server within the department.

This is my personal site. Students may wish to visit my teaching site, https://learning-fin.tech, which includes access to my online textbook. My teaching site also includes documentation for the ether-real-estate module that I wrote to generate a real estate investment game over blockchain transactions; students with a bit more Python experience may want to try their hand at the game!
